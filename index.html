About me

I'm a self-taught developer who started down this path in the fall of 2019, after I moved to Amsterdam from San Francisco. I love learning new things, so I started learning Dutch and (re-learning) programming at the same time.

Previous work

I'm even prouder of the positive impact we had on thousands of people around the world who improved their lives with money earned through our platform.
